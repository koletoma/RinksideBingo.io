Definitions

"Double-Double" -> Two players are wearing the same jersey number and are on the ice at the same time.

"Blood!" -> A player is in the lineup and blood can be seen on his person.

"Rookie" -> A rookie ( a player who has yet to complete a full NHL season ) get time on ice during the game.

"Lost Fan" -> The broadcast camera or jumbotron shows an attending fan who's wearing a jersey which does not belong to either team.

"Own Goal" -> A goal is scored on team Z, having a player from team Z (golies not included) be the last to touch the puck.
